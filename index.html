FIND - Your Personal Financial Advisor, Always at Your Service

Abstract

The AI-Based Financial Advisor website is an innovative platform designed to help individuals manage their finances and investments more effectively. By utilizing AI and machine learning, the website provides personalized financial plans, tailored investment recommendations, detailed expense tracking, and goal-setting tools. The advisor aims to empower users with actionable insights and strategies to achieve their financial goals and ensure long-term stability. With features like real-time data integration, dynamic plan updates, and user-friendly interfaces, the AI-Based Financial Advisor offers a comprehensive solution for modern financial management. For our Capstone Project, we are working on FIND - Revolutionizing Financial Guidance with AI-Powered Personal Advisors which aims to facilitate individuals in effectively managing their finances and investments. FIND helps individuals to solve any of their financial concerns and addresses the growing need for personalized financial guidance. While our financial goals are increasing, our project helps users make better decisions and reach their goals in the best way possible. Our website chatbot offers various suggestions to users based on their input and choice for managing debt or investing in various fields. It also advises on "How to save their money or source income in the best way for any future use".

Our Services

  • Personalized Financial Management: Tailor financial plans based on user data.
  • AI-Driven Investment Recommendations: Offer personalized investment suggestions.
  • Expense Tracking: Automatically track and categorize expenses.
  • Goal Setting: Assist users in setting and achieving financial goals.

Existing Methods

To differentiate our Financial Advisor Website from existing solutions, we analyzed these popular applications: Financely and FinAI. While these platforms provide financial guidance, our project introduces unique elements that make it stand out.

  • Financely: An AI-based financial advisor and user portfolio management system that demystifies trading and the stock market, making them accessible to those without a formal education in finance.
  • FinAI SME Advisor: A Flask application utilizing GPT-3.5 Turbo, it provides financial and business advice for SMEs, focusing on loan queries and predictive analytics, deployed conveniently with features like loan approval predictions and business idea generation.

Modules

Our methodology integrates Web Development tools to optimize AI-powered financial advice with personalized accuracy for the users. For storing user information, chat histories, and financial data securely, Local Storage is used, and the Reinforcement Learning technique is employed to continuously improve the chatbot based on user interactions. The chatbot is also trained to provide user feedback integration to improve response quality through user satisfaction scoring.

A Gamification Points and Rewards System methodology is also integrated into our website, which builds competition between users and encourages them to manage their finances effectively. By utilizing web development and AI tools, our chatbot offers personalized and responsive financial advice. This setup ensures that it effectively learns from interactions, providing high-quality, consistent user experiences.

The gamification approach for the FIND website aims to enhance user engagement and financial literacy by incorporating interactive, rewarding elements that motivate users to reach their financial goals. Gamified features like progress tracking, reward systems, and milestone achievements are designed to provide a sense of accomplishment and improve user retention.

Our Reflections

The FIND Financial Advisor Website successfully brings a new, accessible, and personalized approach to financial management through its AI-driven chatbot. Designed to empower users with practical, tailored advice, FIND bridges the gap between expensive financial advisory services and those who lack access to expert guidance. The platform's intuitive interface, data-driven insights, and advanced learning capabilities ensure that users receive timely, accurate, and relevant recommendations to achieve their financial goals.

As it continuously evolves through user feedback and algorithm improvements, FIND stands as a valuable tool for individuals looking to take control of their finances. By focusing on affordability, privacy, and user experience, it democratizes financial planning and enhances financial literacy for a diverse audience. The project has demonstrated the potential to transform personal finance management, offering a user-friendly, reliable, and innovative solution to real-world financial challenges.
